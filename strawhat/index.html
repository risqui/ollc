ABOUT THE STRAW HAT PIRATES

The infamous and ambitious Straw Hat Pirates, also known as the Mugiwara Pirates, Straw Hat Crew, or simply the Straw Hats, are a pirate group that first appeared in the East Blue. They are the main subject and protagonists of the One Piece manga and anime. A diverse group of people with a range of ambitions and goals, whose dreams inevitably become one as they freely explore even the ends of the world, withstanding it all together; are captained by Monkey D.
